About Gelato Studios

At Gelato Studios, we have a highly experienced team of wrappers looking to compliment and make the look of your vehicle unique. We are able to enhance the looks of your vehicle with wrapping. We work with anything from simple details such as roof & mirrors to full vehicle wrap.

We offer a range of premium bespoke services from Vehicle Wrapping, Window Tinting, Custom Builds, Vehicle Conversions, Leather Interiors and much more.

We are located in Hertfordshire, just 15 minutes away from London.

For more information or to book your vehicle in with us please contact us via e-mail or telephone.
